# Pamela: Festival Floor

A tiny standalone, single-file browser game. You play **Pamela**, the event organizer running **Startupfest 2026** at Le Grand Quai in Montréal — keep the floor alive from doors-open to close without burning out.

▶️ **Play it:** https://alistaircroll.github.io/Pamelafest/

## How to play
- **Click the boardwalk** to walk; **click a pavilion** to head there. Each pavilion's coloured pad on the floor is its spot.
- **Solve crises** — the bubbles over pavilions — before their countdown runs out. Grow Buzz 🔥, keep the Crowd 😀 happy, attract Investors 🤝.
- **Recharge** Pamela's Energy ⚡ / Caffeine ☕ / Comfort 🚻 at the matching floor stations.
- **Claim or fix pop-up events** by walking to their square: green = claim the upside, red = mitigate the trouble before the timer ends.
- Survive **09:00 → 18:00** for a scored grade. Let the Crowd hit zero and the conference collapses.

Keyboard: **WASD/arrows** nudge · **P** pause · **M** mute · **H** help.

## Tech
One self-contained `index.html` — no build step, no dependencies, no external files. Rendered on a 2D `<canvas>`, system fonts only. Just open it in any modern browser.
